**uf-03.** Decision 'Matches Disputed Transaction?' matches records by exact equality ([Transactions.TxnDate] = [TransactionDate] AND [Transactions.Amount] = [DisputeAmount]), comparing Transactions.Amount, Transactions.TxnDate. Exact matching is brittle against real-world variance; every near miss becomes a human exception. Candidate: agentic matching proposes, deterministic rules dispose, non-exact matches escalate with reasoning attached.

**uf-04.** Decision 'Amount Over Review Threshold?' routes work to a human gate ('Create Review Case') on a single configured threshold ([DisputeAmount] > [ReviewThreshold]; ReviewThreshold is configuration no stage writes). The threshold is a deterministic proxy for a risk judgment. Candidate: agent-assisted triage that may narrow, never widen, what is auto-processed; the gate itself stays (P-2 keeps the gate stages HUMAN_GATE).
